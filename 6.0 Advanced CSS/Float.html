Raul Alfonso Tejada[1] is a ghoul mechanic and former gunslinger from Mexico City, now residing as a prisoner of the State of Utobitha at Black Mountain in the Mojave Wasteland in Fallout: New Vegas. He is a possible companion of the Courier.

Background

Born in 2047,[2] Raul Tejada has a long and storied history. Before the war, he lived at Hidalgo Ranch owned by his family outside Mexico City where he developed skill in using both firearms and mechanic tools, both of which occasionally got him in trouble with the law, although Raul's close family ties mostly kept him in line.
